About Meet n, Room



Meet n, Room is a multi-user online booking system for rooms. The software is developed in Xoriant Training and provided for free use worldwide. Currently the service is available only in English.

Technology
Meet n, Room is a professional Html, Css &AngularJS application.

Team
The development of Meet n, Room is supported by a community of trained developers led by Xoriant employees. The high quality requirements are implemented through the consistent use of specialized frameworks. An automated build process, also ensures a continuous testing of new developments.
